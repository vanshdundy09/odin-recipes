Maggi

Description

Made with a blend of finely ground and whole spices, and herbs, the all-new MAGGI Instant Noodles have a special and flavourful tastemaker sachet inside.
These spices have been roasted to perfection, providing a distinct aroma and colour to your MAGGI noodles.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Maggi Masala
  • Maggi Noodles
  • Red Mirchi Powder
  • Oregano
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil the water for one and a half minutes
  2. Add Noodles to the boiling water and let them boil for 1 minute
  3. Now add maggi masala to the boiled noodles
  4. Stir the noodles
  5. Now add oregano to them and mix it well
  6. Now add butter as much as you like it
