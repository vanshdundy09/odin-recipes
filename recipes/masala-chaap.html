Masala Chaap

Description

Masala chaap is a very delicious dish made from soya. It is not a source of protein for those of you fools thinks it is.
Kindly do not eat this and eat sunny leone chaap only.

Ingredients

  • Soya chaap
  • Masala
  • Nimbu
  • Mirchi

Steps

  1. Mujhe nhi pata bhai.
  2. Mai khud veerji malai chaap par jaa ke khaata hoon.
  3. Aur bas sunny leone chaap khaata hoon.
  4. Kyunki wahi sabse best hai.
