Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is toasted bread—usually French bread or baguette—topped with either butter or olive oil, garlic, herbs, and sometimes cheese.
Garlic bread is derived from Italian bruschetta and is typically served alongside Italian foods like pasta, lasagna, or pizza.

Ingredients

  • Bread 2 pieces
  • Cheese
  • Garlic powder
  • Chilli flakes
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the garlic powder well with the butter.
  2. Apply it on the bread pieces.
  3. Now but some butter on the pan and let it melt.
  4. Now put the cheese inside the bread pieces.
  5. Now finally put the bread pieces inside the pan and keep on flipping them.
  6. Top it with some chilli flakes
